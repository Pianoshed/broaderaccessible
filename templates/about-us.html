About Us

Broader Accessible-Health Initiatives (BAHI) is a registered, not-for-profit, and non-political organization dedicated to advancing equitable healthcare access and strengthening health systems across Nigeria. Founded on the principles of accountability, collaboration, and impact-driven service, BAHI works to bridge critical gaps in healthcare delivery—particularly for vulnerable and underserved populations.

Our mission is to provide sustainable, evidence-based interventions that enhance the efficiency of health governance, service delivery, and community health outcomes. We focus on key thematic areas including maternal and child health, reproductive health and family planning, malaria prevention and treatment, and the prevention and management of HIV/AIDS.

Through strategic partnerships with government agencies, local communities, and international development partners, we support healthcare facilities at both state and local levels with technical assistance, advocacy, and targeted program implementation. We are committed to empowering healthcare systems with the tools and resources needed to deliver quality, people-centered care.

Our Mission & Core Values

Our Mission

To enhance equitable access to quality healthcare by strengthening health systems, supporting evidence-based interventions, and empowering communities through strategic partnerships that promote sustainable, people-centered health outcomes.

Equity

We believe everyone deserves access to quality healthcare regardless of gender, location, or socio-economic status.

Integrity

We uphold transparency, accountability, and ethical standards in all our operations and partnerships.

Collaboration

We foster inclusive partnerships with governments, communities, and stakeholders to drive sustainable impact.

Innovation

We embrace data-driven, context-specific solutions to improve healthcare delivery and system performance.

Compassion

We prioritize the dignity, well-being, and voices of the vulnerable and underserved in all our programs.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering high-quality, measurable outcomes that align with global health goals and local priorities.

Founder's Message

Dr. Isaac Olugbenga Aladeniyi

Medical & Field Epidemiologist | Director, Family Health

Welcome to Broader Accessible-Health Initiatives. As a medical and field epidemiologist, my passion has always been rooted in using science and strategic leadership to transform health outcomes — particularly in the areas of non-communicable diseases and maternal and child healthcare.

Working as a senior health systems specialist in Nigeria's subnational government, I have witnessed first-hand the critical gaps in service delivery, policy alignment, and public health equity. This has only deepened my commitment to building resilient systems that are data-informed, people-centered, and sustainably financed.

At BAHI, our work is guided by integrity, evidence, and empathy. We are not just delivering programs — we are restoring dignity, empowering communities, and creating a healthier future for all. I invite you to be a part of this journey — whether through partnership, advocacy, volunteering, or support.

Together, we can make healthcare more accessible, inclusive, and impactful.

BAHI is committed to improving healthcare accessibility and outcomes through innovation, evidence-based policies, and community-driven initiatives.
