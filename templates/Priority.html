Innovative Programs for Health Equity and Impact

We design and implement strategic health programs that respond to the needs of communities and deliver measurable results.

Integrated Health Solutions That Change Lives

From malaria to maternal care, our programs address the full spectrum of public health priorities with a focus on access and sustainability.

Infectious Diseases

Preventing, detecting, and responding to infectious disease threats that affect communities.

Malaria

Comprehensive prevention, diagnosis, and treatment to reduce malaria-related deaths and illness.

HIV/AIDS

Targeted interventions for prevention, care, treatment, and stigma reduction in affected populations.

Non-Communicable Diseases

Addressing chronic diseases through education, lifestyle interventions, and access to care.

Reproductive Health & Family Planning

Empowering individuals with access to modern contraceptives, informed choices, and services that support reproductive rights and healthy families.

Maternal and Child Health

We ensure safe pregnancies, skilled delivery, and newborn care through community outreach and facility support, reducing preventable maternal and child deaths.

Nutrition

Ensuring improved nutrition outcomes for children, women, and vulnerable groups through targeted interventions.

Our Projects in Action

BAHI is committed to improving healthcare accessibility and outcomes through innovation, evidence-based policies, and community-driven initiatives.
