Who we are

BRAIN is dedicated to advancing sustainable, evidence-based health interventions that strengthen governance, service delivery, and community health systems. Our core focus areas include maternal and child health, reproductive health and family planning, malaria prevention, and HIV/AIDS care and prevention.

Through strategic partnerships and community engagement, we contribute to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being. We also emphasize strengthening primary health care and community systems to ensure inclusive and equitable access to quality care.

What we do

BRAIN strengthens health and social systems through evidence-based solutions. We focus on maternal and child health, reproductive health, malaria, HIV/AIDS, and reinforce primary and community health care for sustainable impact.
